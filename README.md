# 🛒 Retail Sales Analysis

## 📊 Project Overview
This project analyzes retail sales data using Power BI.

The main goal is to understand sales performance, product categories, and demand patterns.

---

## 📁 Dataset
The dataset includes:
- Order data
- Sales
- Categories and sub-categories
- Regions
- Dates

Data was cleaned and prepared in Excel.

---

## 🧠 Analysis

### ABC Analysis
Products are divided into:
- A: high revenue
- B: medium revenue
- C: low revenue

### XYZ Analysis
Products are divided by demand stability:
- X: stable demand
- Y: medium variability
- Z: high variability

### ABC-XYZ Matrix
Combination of ABC and XYZ to identify product segments.

---

## 📈 Dashboard

The dashboard includes:
- Total Sales
- Total Orders
- Average Order Value
- Sales by Category
- Sales by Region
- Sales Trend
- Top 10 Products
- ABC-XYZ Matrix

---

## 🔍 Key Insights

- A category products generate most of the revenue  
- No stable demand products were found (no X segment)  
- Sales are concentrated in a few categories  
- Some products fall into risky segments (CZ)
